  • High Concept: Move platform to platform as a long cat who can stretch himself!
  • Theme: Stretching
  • Mandated Variety: Mouse input, randomness, competitor, side-scroller
  • Prototype Goal: This is a prototype for a mobile game, seeing if the mechanic presented would be fun to play to get a higher and higher score.
  • Player Experience Goals: I want them to start off pretty bad at the game, and as they get better at timing improve their score and try to get an even higher score.
  • Gameplay: The player can preform only 1 action, and that is to hold down their mouse until they feel that the cat is long enough to reach the next platform. If they under or over shoot it, then the cat dies and your score resets to 0. Your goal is to keep trying to reach a higher score, similar to flappy bird. As you play more you will get better and better at the timing.
  • Strategies: The game is purely timing, there is little to no strategy involved. Perhaps power ups could be added? Or streak bonuses?
  • Story/Setting/Premise: There really is no back story, except the main character is a meme Long Cat
  • Target Audience: Mobile game users who are trying to kill a couple minutes at a time.
  • Play Time: Depends how long until the user gets bored, can vary from a few minutes to maybe 10 at most.
