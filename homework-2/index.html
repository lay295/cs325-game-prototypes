  • High Concept: You're a park ranger and pesky fires keep starting, drive around in your car to put out the fires!
  • Theme: national park service ranger
  • Mandated Variety: Keyboard, a lot of randomness, achiver, overhead
  • Prototype Goal: Trying to see if a top down speed game with a high score would be fun
  • Player Experience Goals: To be able to beat their previous high score
  • Gameplay: The actions the player can preform are moving in 4 directions with their keyboard keys, and their task is to put out fires in the forest because they're a forest ranger.
  • Strategies: Finding effective methods to comb the forest to find the tree that has been on fire.
  • Story/Setting/Premise: You are just a normal forest ranger trying to put out fires. Perhaps I could change it so the main character would be smokey the bear.
  • Target Audience: This could be well suited and adapted to be a mobile game, which can be a very quick game
